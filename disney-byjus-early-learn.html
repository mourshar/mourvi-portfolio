BYJU'S × Disney Early Learn - Senior Designer

Byju's · 2018-2020

Senior Learning Designer

Overview

Disney BYJU'S Early Learn is a subscription-based learning experience for children ages 3–8, built to teach early math and English through playful, story-driven adventures. As Senior Learning Designer, I played a key role in shaping the educational foundation of the platform and integrating 23 Disney IPs into BYJU's flagship early-learning ecosystem.

This collaboration-between one of the world's most recognised EdTech companies (valued at $22.5B in 2022) and Disney's beloved entertainment universe-marked a new benchmark for curriculum-aligned learning games at scale.

My Role & Contributions

Platform-Level Learning & UX Architecture

I designed the learning and interaction architecture across the entire app, working with UX designers, game designers, and artists. This included:

  • Creating navigation flows built for ages 3–8, accounting for early motor skills, attention spans, and cognitive load.
  • Designing the UX architecture and recommendation engine, enabling personalised lesson pathways for millions of learners.
  • Building systems for adaptive content delivery, using engagement signals and learning progression data.

Parent Experience

Because parents are key decision-makers in early learning products, I designed a dedicated parent layer that:

  • Offers clear insights into a child's progress and milestones
  • Provides offline, everyday activities tied to in-app learning
  • Strengthens family engagement around learning

Learning Design Across Domains

I created learning templates and interaction patterns that powered thousands of lesson activities, including:

  • Early numeracy tasks (counting, number recognition, composition)
  • Foundational English skills (phonological awareness, beginning/ending sounds)
  • Child-appropriate feedback loops and scaffolding mechanisms

Every design decision centred on developmental appropriateness, attention patterns, and the need for high engagement in short bursts.

Personalisation & Adaptive Learning

My work shaped the platform's adaptive engine by:

  • Analysing each child's abilities, pace, and interests
  • Delivering personalised activity sequences
  • Dynamically adjusting difficulty and content mix
  • Balancing mastery-based progression with character-driven appeal

This system became a cornerstone of the app's stickiness and educational efficacy.
