Overview

Early Math with Gracie & Friends is a suite of playful math games for children ages 3–5 that blend early numeracy, physical movement, and collaborative play. The series helped young learners build foundational skills through active, joyful experiences grounded in early childhood development.

Design Approach

Early Math: counting, one-to-one correspondence, patterns, spatial skills

Movement: kinesthetic learning woven into gameplay

Collaboration: cooperative interactions that encouraged communication and teamwork

Design Philosophy

  • Learning through play
  • Active bodies enhance active thinking
  • Social contexts deepen learning
  • Positive early math experiences build long-term confidence

Game Examples

  • Lemonade Stand: playful pretend-business math
  • Birthday Café: matching, counting, and patterns
  • City Skate: spatial sequencing through movement
