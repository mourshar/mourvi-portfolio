Currently at Meta

I'm Mourvi Sharma - an Experience Designer & Product Innovator who builds scalable content systems and the stories that power them across AI products, games, and learning experiences.

Over the past 15 years, I've led cross-functional, first-of-their-kind initiatives at Meta, Disney, Sesame Workshop, IBM Research, BYJU'S, and my own award-winning studio, BigFatPhoenix Interactive.

At Meta, I design prompt frameworks, evaluation pipelines, and experimentation loops that help AI characters come alive at scale - taking multi-AI content from 100 to 1,000 pieces in just three months, and turning real-time signal from experiments into data-driven content strategy. Before that, I co-founded BigFatPhoenix, raised $1.3M in venture funding, and led the creation of Fairside Stories - a teacher-certified SEL game co-designed with 500+ kids, later adapted by the Government of Tamil Nadu to encourage youth civic participation.

My roots are in storytelling and learning design. At Disney, I helped create award-winning learning apps like Mickey's Magical Math World - which hit #1 in 80+ countries - and at Sesame Workshop, I led literacy and media projects that reached over a million children in low-resource communities through radio, mobile, and school programmes.

What ties it all together is a belief that creativity and technology are at their best when they serve people - when they teach, include, and spark delight. My work sits at that intersection: data-driven, culturally grounded, and built to scale meaningfully.

Currently exploring opportunities to design the next generation of AI-powered experiences. Let's create something impactful together.
